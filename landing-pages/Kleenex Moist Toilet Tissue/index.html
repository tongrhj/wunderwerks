FAQ

Product queries

  1. Q:How many sheets should I use?

    A: We recommend a use of 1 – 2 sheets each time.

  2. Q:Should I use this product before or after using dry toilet tissue?

    A:As part of the Kleenex® Clean Routine, we suggest moist toilet tissue to be used after dry toilet tissue, to leave you feeling cleaner and fresher.

  3. Q:Where is this product manufactured?

    A:Our Kleenex® Ultra Soft Moist Toilet Tissues are manufactured in Korea.

  4. Q:I have sensitive skin. Is this product suitable for my use?

    A:Yes, our product is hypoallergenic tested and suitable for users with sensitive skin.

  5. Q:Can I use this as a normal wet tissue?

    A:This product has been specially formulated for usage as a toilet tissue with its flushability properties.

  6. Q:Will the moist toilet tissue clog the toilet if I flush it down with toilet paper?

    A:Our wipes have undergone a flushability test that meets the INDA/EDANA guidelines. We would suggest that the wet wipes are flushed one sheet at a time for best results.

  7. Q:Where can I buy this?

    A:Kleenex® Ultra Soft Moist Toilet Tissues can be purchased at major super/hypermarkets.

  8. Q:Is this product safe for use for intimate areas?

    A:Yes, our products have undergone a product safety assessment process and is safe for use on intimate areas.

  9. Q:If I have any product queries, who can I contact?

    A:You may contact us at 1800-338 8838.

Sample request information

  1. Q:How long will it take for me to get my sample?

    A:It will take approximately 2-3 weeks for the sample to reach you.

  2. Q:How can I track the status of my sample?

    A:Please drop us an email at kc@letrain.com.sg for sample related enquiries.
