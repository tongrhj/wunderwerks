Introducing the Cocomo App

Your one-stop access to the largest number of trusted spas in Singapore
and the region.



Reliable spas

Your safety is our priority and all our partners are curated and vetted to ensure that we provide only the best possible service for you.

Convenience at your fingertips

Our Simulcast system detects your location and identifies spas in your vicinity instantly. With bookings guaranteed within minutes, arrange for a pampering session wherever you are, whenever you want.

Clear pricing

All rates listed by our partners do not contain any hidden costs. What you see is what you pay.

Secure, cashless payments

All payments are handled electronically and securely so there's no need for cash when you book with us.

Rewards & deals

Earn points with every transaction you make and redeem them for discounts and premiums. Save your favourite spas and receive notifications of any surprise offers and deals.

How it works

Request

We'll broadcast your request and preferences to a host of
recommended spas. Spas will be given 30 minutes to respond with an offer.

Confirm

Accept an offer and make payment to confirm your booking.

Be pampered

You're done! Simply head down to your selected spa and enjoy your pampering session.
