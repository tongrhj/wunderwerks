Meet the Speakers

General Session

John Shewchuk
Technical Fellow, Microsoft

Tackling the growing complexity in software development

John Shewchuk is a Technical Fellow who leads the DX engineering and technical evangelism team at Microsoft. A 20-year veteran of Microsoft, John has worked on all kinds of projects including – driving the first release of Visual Studio, building Azure Active Directory and the Microsoft graph, streaming the Olympics with NBC, and solving challenges for cloud healthcare solutions with GE. John is an advocate and contributor to open source and Web standards; most recently he was involved with offline data modules for Angular and the OAuth 2 standards.

Twitter: @JohnShew

Dr. Graham Williams
Director of Data Science, Cloud and Enterprise,
Microsoft Singapore

Machine Learning and Data Science: Data into intelligent action

Graham Williams is Microsoft's Director of Data Science for the APAC region. He recently joined Microsoft bringing with him over 30 years of research and deployment in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Analytics and Data Science. Graham is also an author of a number of publications focusing on the introduction of Data Mining and Machine Learning using the R Statistical Open Source Software.

Fang Zhou
Data Scientist, Cloud and Enterprise,
Microsoft Singapore

Machine Learning and Data Science: Data into intelligent action

Fang Zhou is a Data Scientist at Microsoft’s IMML Algorithms & Data Science team. She holds a Ph.D. degree in Statistics from National University of Singapore and has domain knowledge and experiences in marketing analysis, risk & finance, and healthcare. She is currently responsible for building advanced analytics/machine learning powered intelligent web-services and end-to-end solutions for customers in diverse verticals.
