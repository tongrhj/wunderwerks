MC36 IS BUILT FOR BUSINESS, YET EASY AS A SMARTPHONE

The MC36 is built for life in the field. An enterprise-class device designed for business but every bit as easy to use as a consumer smartphone, it gives your workers instant access to information to improve productivity and customer service quality.

It is the only device in its class with dual wireless network connectivity operating on Android™. The MC36 also features best in class large display for outdoor readability and the most battery capacity for more mileage. Plus, great memory space, scan engine options, and right and left-handed compatibility for more efficiency.

The MC36 is the perfect solution to keep your employees on the road connected, near or far.
