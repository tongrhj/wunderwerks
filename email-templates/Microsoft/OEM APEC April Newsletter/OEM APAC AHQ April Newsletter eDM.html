Device Excite

Malaysia - Device Days

The Malaysia leg of the APAC Device Days was held in Kuala Lumpur and Penang on the 3rd and 17th of March respectively. The Malaysia team took this opportunity to equip the attendees with objection handling techniques, practical tips to demo Windows 10, and advice on turning mis-licensed situations to opportunities. Attendees also had the opportunity to learn and experience the latest Windows Pro devices from the JOI, Acer, Asus, Dell, HP and Lenovo representatives.

The event was a success, attracting approximately 200 attendees, including distributors, managed partners and resellers. The team also collected 68 potential leads for the “Breadth Acceleration and Transformation” breadth reseller program.

Indonesia – New CTE Partner: Megatroniz Mitraniaga

Megatronix Mitraniaga. our new CTE partner in Indonesia, launched their first device, Venturer Bravowin10K, in March. With almost 25 years of experience as a distributor to Ncomputing, Zconverter and Epson, Megatronix hopes to capture the entry level segment market with their new device, a Windows 10 2-in-1 detachable notebook.

As part of the launch promotion, end-users can pre-order the device, exclusively on Bukalapak.com, for Rp2,699,000 (approximately USD$200). This price includes a 3G modem and is only valid from 23 March to 31 March. Venturer will also be available soon at several online stores and retailers in Indonesia.

Event and Initiative Updates

Singapore – Continual effort in OPP & O365 amplification: Lenovo IdeaPad 100S Telco Win
Following the OPP&O365 amplification events shared in the January newsletter, the Singapore OEM team landed an OPP deal with local telco Starhub. For just SGD $79 per month, consumers were able to purchase a Starhub HomeHub 500 along with a Lenovo IdeaPad 100S during the promotion period (18 February to 23 February). More than 1000 units of Lenovo Ideapad 100S with Office 365 professional were sold during the 6-day promotion. The Singapore team hopes to continue this momentum and attach Office 365 Professional to more of the OPP devices.


Malaysia - BAT Partner Night

The APAC Breadth Acceleration Transformation (BAT) program is an initiative to build a breadth reseller community through an engine comprising telesales and a digital engagement portal. The Malaysia OEM team went the extra mile and organized a BAT Partner Night event to engage and reward existing resellers, as well as recruit new members. The team also took this opportunity to share more about the program, the benefits of selling Genuine Windows as well as CTE devices.

The event was a huge success. More than 100 breadth resellers attended the event, out of which 26 of them activated their accounts on the spot. The attendees were appreciative of Microsoft’s effort and focus on the breadth reseller community in Malaysia and were keen to continue their support for the BAT program as well as Microsoft products.


Thailand – Windows 10 Devices on LamNa Show

To drive awareness for Window 10 devices, the Thailand OEM team secured a 15-min slot on the LamNa Show, a popular TV show in Thailand on the latest technology trends. The weekly viewership for the show is approximately 500,000. During the show, the hosts introduced key features of Windows 10 including Windows Hello, Cortana and inking on Microsoft Edge. They also had the opportunity to showcase the Acer AIO Z3-700, Asus UX202, HP Elite X2, Lenovo Miix 700 and Dell XPS 12.

Apart from the TV show, the video was also posted on Techoffside.com, Sanook.com and YouTube. Social media platforms such as Twitter and Facebook were used to drive traffic to the online videos and it proved to be a success. The online video reached more than 13,000 viewers on YouTube alone.


Indonesia – Genuine Windows

In March, the Indonesian Police seized hundreds of counterfeit Windows software and Certificates of Authenticity (CoA), illegally traded by VJ and MJ - stores in the largest IT shopping center in Mangga Dua. Along with the Indonesia Anti- Counterfeit Society (MIAP), the police took legal action against those allegedly involved in the distribution and sale of the counterfeit software.

The Indonesian OEM team took this opportunity to educate and emphasize to partners the importance of selling genuine software by sharing the news articles and materials regarding law regulation and the benefits of using genuine software.
