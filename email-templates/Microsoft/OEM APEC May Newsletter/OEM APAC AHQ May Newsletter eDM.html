Device Excite

APAC - GDP Asia Tech Day

On 9th May, the Global Device Partners (GDP) APAC team held a Tech Day in Singapore to share the latest consumer and commercial device portfolios across the represented OEMs (Asus, Fujitsu, Panasonic, Samsung and Toshiba). Attendees also had the chance to learn about Fujitsu servers as well as IoT solutions and its usage scenarios which these OEMs offer.

The session ended with a device showcase, which allowed attendees were able to have a hands-on experience with the newly launched Apple compete devices. The device showcase included consumer devices such as Samsung TabPro S, Asus ROG GX700 gaming PC and the UX360 premium notebook, as well as commercial devices, such as Fujitsu Q506 for the education segment, Toshiba Portege Z20t and the Panasonic Tough Book CF20.

Malaysia – ThunderMatch’s STOM Launch

On 12th April, ThunderMatch Technology (TMT), Malaysia’s largest IT chain store launched its very own device brand, STOM. The first two STOM devices unveiled were the Spectrum i100 detachable 2-in-1, and the PS01F PC Stick.

With the support of the Malaysia OEM team, ThunderMatch launched a series of brand awareness activities, starting with a media launch event. The event saw 36 media representatives resulting in 19 publications within a week of the event. Following the event, participants in an online contest got a chance to win a Spectrum i100 by highlighting its top 3 features and how it enables them in their work and play. The contest was viewed by over 31K people, of which 311 participated in the contest.

ThunderMatch also offered a launch promotion for the first 500 units of each of the device - RM100 discount for the 2-in-1 and RM200 discount for the PC stick. Consumers will be able to purchase the devices at any of the 93 TMT retail stores, TMT online stores, as well as Lazada.com.

Event and Initiative Updates

APAC – Microsoft Analyst Summit Asia 2016

At the Microsoft Analyst Summit Asia 2016, held from 25-27 April, Microsoft hosted more than 91 analysts from 12 leading analyst firms across the region. The theme of the event was Fuelling Customer Digital Transformation through Innovation.

As part of the Solution Showcase for the verticals, OEM devices such as the Acer B117 for Education, Asus GX700 for Gaming, HP Elite X3 and Samsung Tab Pro S were highlighted. There was also a dedicated table for CTE devices. The analysts were pleasantly surprised with the price point of Emdoor’s i12 ruggedized solution and Flytech’s POS solution. Unique form factors and designs such as eHuge’s Hello Kitty themed 2-in-1, Grace 10 Light, and Exertis’ Linx Vision Gaming Tablet also drew crowds to the CTE table.

Awards

Q3 CCG Obsession Award Winners

Join us in congratulating Jin Ho and Joel for winning the Q3 CCG Obsession Award 2016! The CCG Obsession Award is presented to individuals and teams around the world who are doing outstanding, innovative work. The awards are categorized by Product Love, Partner Love, Consumer Love, Living the Culture and Leadership Excellence.

Partner Love Award

Jin Ho Song
Korea
OEM Field 		Jin Ho has delivered outstanding ODR results, beating budget and forecast, despite a declining market. The SBC market in Korea declined sharply due to rapid channel shift from top SBCs to Named. With effective account targeting and partner programs, Korea met the Windows Client ODR YTD budget as well as forecast, with only a -5% YoY as compared to a 20% decline in market.

Jin Ho also grew BOS Attach to a new high of 38% through focus on Genuine Windows campaigns as well as partnering online channel to drive BOS attach. His focus in driving Windows Pro Commercial devices also resulted sales to increase by more than 10 times YoY.




Partner Love Award

Joel Guzman
Philippines
OEM Field 		In just eight months with the team, Joel not only managed to earn partners’ trust but made a positive impact in Windows, O365, Server and devices.

His close partnership with MNAs and segments resulted in 91% Windows attach, with all MNAs growing through the SEAP program. ASUS in particular had 100% Windows attach rate! Lenovo too, landed 9k units O365 with OPP devices.

Other than engagement with MNAs, Joel also actively drives tablet activation programs and server ROK attach programs with ODR, Retail and Online channels.
