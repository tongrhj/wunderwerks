Introducing the iMZ™ Series for the mobile workforce

Each compact iMZ printer can be a smart assistant for any mobile receipt-printing application where quick, simple receipts are needed on demand.

These unobtrusive printers can be worn comfortably for a full shift without interfering with the user’s tasks.

It is a great first step for businesses looking to replace pen-and-paper documentation, preprinted receipts, or unreliable mobile printers.
COMPATIBLE WITH ALL PLATFORMS

Get the best out of today’s smartphone and tablet devices with the new iMZ™ printers. They now support Bluetooth® connectivity to Apple® iOS, as well as Android™, Windows® Mobile and BlackBerry® platforms.
